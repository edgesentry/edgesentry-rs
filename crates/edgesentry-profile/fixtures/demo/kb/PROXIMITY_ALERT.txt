Site Safety Procedure §3.1 — Powered Industrial Truck Clearance

Powered industrial trucks (forklifts, reach trucks, pallet jacks) must maintain a minimum
clearance of 5 metres from pedestrians and stationary workers at all times while in motion.

Where site conditions prevent maintaining 5 m clearance, the operator must:
  (a) reduce speed to walking pace (≤ 1.5 m/s),
  (b) activate the audible proximity warning, and
  (c) establish eye contact with the pedestrian before proceeding.

Failure to maintain clearance is a recordable near-miss event and must be reported to the
site safety officer within 2 hours.

Reference: ISO 3691-1:2011 Industrial trucks — Safety requirements and verification.
