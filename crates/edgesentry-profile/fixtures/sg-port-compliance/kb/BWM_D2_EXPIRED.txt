Ballast Water Management Convention (BWM) D-2 Standard

The International Maritime Organization (IMO) Ballast Water Management Convention requires
vessels to meet the D-2 performance standard, limiting the number of viable organisms in
discharged ballast water. Singapore MPA enforces compliance via Port Marine Circular No. 19 of 2023.

Key requirements:
- All vessels entering Singapore waters must hold a valid BWM Certificate
- The certificate must not be expired at the time of port entry
- Vessels with expired certificates may be detained or denied port entry
- BWM treatment system must be type-approved and operational

Renewal process:
- Apply to flag state administration at least 3 months before expiry
- Survey by recognised classification society required
- Updated IBWM certificate issued upon successful survey

Reference: IMO MEPC.300(72) — BWM Convention D-2 Standard
MPA Port Marine Circular No. 19 of 2023
