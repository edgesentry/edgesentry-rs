Singapore Quarantine Pre-Notification Requirement

Under the Singapore Environmental Public Health Act and related port health regulations,
vessels arriving at Singapore ports must submit a Maritime Declaration of Health (MDH)
and quarantine pre-notification at least 24 hours before arrival.

Requirements:
- Maritime Declaration of Health (MDH) must be submitted via Port+ system
- Health declaration from Master certifying crew health status
- Crew list with nationalities for contact tracing purposes
- Any reportable illnesses or deaths during voyage must be declared

Failure to provide quarantine pre-notification may result in:
- Mandatory anchorage pending health inspection
- Vessel boarding by Port Health Officers
- Detention until clearance is granted

Reference: Singapore Environmental Public Health Act (Cap. 95)
