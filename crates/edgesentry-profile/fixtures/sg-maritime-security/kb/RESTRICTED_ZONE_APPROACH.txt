Restricted Zone Approach — Singapore Port Limits

The Maritime and Port Authority of Singapore (MPA) designates restricted zones around
critical maritime infrastructure including naval bases, oil terminals, and desalination plants.

Clearance requirements:
- Commercial vessels must maintain minimum 0.5 NM clearance from all restricted zone boundaries
- Clearance measured from nearest point of the restricted zone perimeter
- No unauthorised entry permitted regardless of vessel size or flag state

Restricted zone categories:
- Naval and military zones: Tuas Naval Base, Changi Naval Base
- Petrochemical terminals: Jurong Island exclusion areas
- Water intake zones: PUB desalination plant perimeters

Enforcement:
- Singapore Police Coast Guard (PCG) actively patrols restricted zone boundaries
- Vessels approaching within 0.5 NM are hailed by VHF Channel 16
- Entry without authorisation is a criminal offence under the Maritime and Port Authority Act

Reference: MPA Port Marine Notice No. 79 of 2020
Maritime and Port Authority of Singapore Act (Cap. 170A)
